# SCRIBE Framework

**Best for:** Iterative, conversational workflows where refinement through dialogue improves results. Complex tasks that benefit from back-and-forth.

## Template Structure

Rewrite the prompt into these sections:

### Specify a Role
Assign the AI a clear professional role — the expertise and perspective it should bring to the task.

### Contextualize
Set the scene — provide all relevant background, constraints, and situational information.

### Responsibility
Define what the AI owns — its specific responsibilities and deliverables for this task. What is it accountable for?

### Instructions
Provide step-by-step guidance — the specific actions to take, in order.

### Banter
Set the conversational tone and explicitly invite iterative refinement. Encourage the AI to ask clarifying questions, propose alternatives, or flag concerns before finalizing. Example: "If anything is unclear, ask before proceeding."

### Evaluate
Define how to assess the output — what criteria should be checked, what quality bar applies, and how to self-review before presenting the result.

## Rules for This Framework
- Banter is the key differentiator — it explicitly builds in dialogue rather than treating the prompt as one-shot
- Evaluate acts as a built-in quality gate
- This framework works best when the user expects to iterate on the result
