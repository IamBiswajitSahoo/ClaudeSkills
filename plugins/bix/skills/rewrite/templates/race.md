# RACE Framework

**Best for:** Fast professional tasks, expert-driven work where role-framing and clear expectations drive quality.

## Template Structure

Rewrite the prompt into these sections:

### Role
Define the expert persona the AI should adopt — the specific expertise, professional background, and perspective needed for the task.

### Action
State the specific task to perform — use a precise action verb and be unambiguous about what needs to be done.

### Context
Provide the relevant background — the situation, project, existing constraints, and any information the AI needs to make good decisions.

### Expectation
Describe what the output should look like — format, quality characteristics, success criteria, or specific deliverables.

## Rules for This Framework
- RACE is lighter than RISEN — use it when you don't need step-by-step decomposition
- Role is the differentiator — it frames the entire response through expert eyes
- Keep each section to 1-3 sentences for maximum impact
